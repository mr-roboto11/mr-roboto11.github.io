People to Avoid in Business

The Naysayer

Just like the name entails, these are the people filled with doubt. Entreprneurs should be filled with ambition, faith, and hope. The naysayers are devoid of these three qualities and/or don't think you are able to achieve your goals. Either way, avoid this type like the black plague because they will fill your mind with doubt. They'll tell you every reason it can't be done and why you can't do it.

"They Will Fill your Mind with Doubt"

-KIN

The Hater

Beware of these jealous mfs. Their feelings come from an evil place. They are mad at you and/or your entreprenuerial endeavors. They will malelovently destroy your character, reputation, business, and your dreams to shreds if you don't avoid them. They are disguised as family, friends, co-workers, and people on your social media friends list. This type is most likely to do some volatile sh*t to you; steal you items, tell blantant disrespectful lies, blame you for things you've never done, and disrespect your business any way they can. Rid your self of these cancerous bunch or you will find everything you work torwards deteriorate.

"Likely to do Some Volatile Sh*t to You"

-KIN

The Credential Checker

"What exactly do you do?"

"Do you have a degree?"

"Do you have an Associates or Bachelors?"

"How long have you been doing what you do?"

Do you know how to do XYZ because that other business knows How?

This type is the living embodiment of "to die by comparison." They will bombard you with an array of pestering questions in an attempt to deem you unworthy. There is no amount of crendentials you can possess to appease their psuedo-inquisitiveness because in their eyes they have already passed judgement. They are just going through their "mental checklist" to confirm it. This type is definetely annoying. Offset their questions with questions; "Do you have a degree?", "Do you have an invoice"

"Do you have an invoice?"

-KIN

Mr & Mrs Me Too

Is that my shadow, my clone, or a doppleganger? No, it's my human carbon copy. Beware of this type especially. They are the sneaks of the bunch who wait around like bear traps. One wrong step and they have your whole business plan. This type may even be a fellow entreprenuer! You'll find them in the Barnes & Noble, Starbucks, the local library, or workspace faking it till they make it. The difference between you and them, is that they never had a dream. They have a hustle they may have copied from someone else. They will jump from industry to industry, mimmicking. Their goal is money, money, money! If your business, endeavors, or goals seem like they may be a commercial success. "Mr and Mrs Me Too" will be right there doing everything you do. Think of them like Covid. Isolate yourself and keep something over your mouth.

"They Will Jump from Industry to Industry"

-KIN

Comedians

Sometimes people laugh to hide the pain. This may be the case with this type. That or they have some serious mental issues. What's so funny about making money? Where's the joke in being an entreprenuer? Why am I a joke to you? Would you want to be around someone who thinks you and your endeavors are a joke? The real joke is that they are sad but instead of addressing whatever is wrong in their boring lives. They have decided to laugh at your entreprenuerial hardships and use it as medicine for their miserable souls. Like the hyenas of the african safari, these scavengers run in packs, cackling at any and everything they can. Steer clear of these clowns or you'll become part of the show.

"Steer Clear of These Clowns"

-KIN

The Poor Minded

Some people have an abundance mindset and some have a scarcity mindset. The poor minded individual will always be poor because of the way they think. This type is a self-sabotaging entity that operates in a scarcity mindset. They don't understand value. They have bad spending habits. They have bad saving habits. They don't have any type of plan. Jesus might have a plan for them but they don't have one for themselves. For these reasons it is imperative you stay away from them. You look like money to them, your business assets look like pawn shop items, not to mention their rent is due.

"You look like Money to Them"

-KIN

The Boomers

This type is possibly the only one that's harmless to be around. Gather by the fire everyone, let me tell you of a tale of when people worked at jobs for 30 years. A land were email was known as mail. Where there was no internet or women getting rich off social media for twerking (...I'm just saying). They won't understand any of your new age entreprenuerial communist voodoo. Although they may have great insight about hustle.

"They May have Great Insight about Hustle"

-KIN

The Simplified

Forest Gump had a great saying, "Stupid is as Stupid Does" or something like that. Meaning, an unintelligent person can only do as much as they know how to do (...if that makes sense). Business is fast. Being an entreprenuer is an intricate thing. Intelligence is required, unless you have the good fortune to delegate all task because you have a lot of capital. Emotional intelligence is also required. So dealing with people who don't possess these qualities will send your dreams crashing and burning. Small minds. Small-minded conversation (ieGossip). Short-sighted (no vision). Inpatient. Loyalty switches upon based on opportunity. Be kind but be smart when dealing with this type.

"Small minds. Small-minded conversation. Short-sighted. Inpatient. Loyalty Switches."

-KIN
